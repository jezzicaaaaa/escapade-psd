how it all started

Escape room is a cool type of entertainment in Stockholm and around the world. Why? Because it doesn’t matter if you are 12 or 70 years old, organizing a team building for a company or just hanging out with your friends – you are guaranteed to have fun. You will be locked inside a room which has it’s own atmosphere and background story.

Your main goal is to find all the hints, solve all the puzzles and escape before time runs out.
